German Version

We're excited to announce that this year's tournament will be held at 01:30 at our apartment. Make sure to be there on time and bring your best game face.

Tournament Details

  1. Handicap Round - Mega Rage Cage
    We'll use 5, 6, or 7 balls depending on how many people sign up.

  2. Random Teams Allocation Beer Pong Round
    We'll put you in teams with random people and make you play against each other until only 25% qualify for the next round.

    For time-constraint reasons we are going to have to use a reduced amount of cups :(

    *Note: If the game takes too long, the organizers reserve the right to settle it with a beer tornado. May the best man or woman win!

  3. Flunky Ball Free for All
    The top 25% will play a round of flunky ball outside in the Coutryad. The first three to win will be declared the winners and will receive prizes, with the first person being declared the King of WG6 Tournament 2023 👑 The last three remaining in Flunky Ball will have to complete a punishment!

    Go to the poll and vote for the punishment you want to see them do!
